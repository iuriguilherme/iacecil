# Question Bank - Intake Questions by Purpose

<do>
- What specific artifact or outcome should be produced?
- What is the scope of the task (files to modify, components to build)?
- Are there existing patterns or code to follow?
- What is the preferred technical approach?
- Are there any specific libraries or dependencies to use?
- What are the success criteria for this task?
</do>

<plan>
- What is the goal of the plan (new feature, refactor, research workflow)?
- What is the preferred format (phases, roadmap, specific structure)?
- Are there any known constraints or deadlines?
- What are the major milestones?
- Who are the stakeholders or target audience for the plan?
- How much detail is needed for each phase?
</plan>

<research>
- What is the depth required (overview vs exhaustive)?
- What are the primary questions to answer?
- Are there specific sources to consult (official docs, specific sites)?
- What is the intended output format (markdown, comparison table, structured XML)?
- Should it include code examples?
- Is there a specific technical stack context?
</research>

<refine>
- What is the specific feedback on the existing output?
- What was missing or insufficient?
- Which areas need more depth?
- What worked well and should be preserved?
- Are there new requirements that have emerged?
- Is the target selection clear?
</refine>
